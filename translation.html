TRANSLATION

After centuries of technological progress and economic growth, the world is becoming more connected than ever. The process of interacting and integration among people, nations, and countries is driving international trades and investments. Globalization provides many opportunities for people from different backgrounds and ethnic groups. Despite the positive outcome of globalization, the interaction of people who speak different languages can be challenging and may lead to some misunderstanding. "Silk Road Multilingual" helps to find solutions by providing certified translation services for various industries. Industries we provide solutions for and examples of documents we translate:

  • General
  • Medical
  • Legal
  • Government
  • Technical
  • Business
  • Website

General:

birth certificates, marriage certificates, passports, driver licenses, diplomas, academic transcripts, official letters, emails, etc.

Medical:

medical history records, discharge summaries, medical test results, mental status examinations, operative reports, treatment histories, medical directives, living wills, advance directives, immunization records, etc.

Legal:

legal contracts, business contracts, child care authorizations, last wills and testaments, divorce settlement agreements, eviction notices, independent contract agreements, etc.

Government:

permanent resident cards, foreign passports, citizenship documents, medical examination records, vaccination records, arrest records, court depositions, etc.

Technical:

patents, user manuals, user instructions, instruction manuals, technical reports, etc.

Business:

memorandums, business letters, business reports, transactional documents, financial documents, company agreements, buy-sell agreements, etc.

Website:

web contents, videos, etc.

Our goal is to assist clients with quality translation services in a short time at a low cost. Highly-qualified and competent professionals translate any documents into target-language quickly while maintaining the original structure and form of a source-language text. Detail-oriented translators pay particular attention to the correctness of name spellings and official seals, localization of dates and numbers, and document formatting. All translated documents are notarized. Certified translators and editors guarantee a hundred percent human translation of a document and its confidentiality.
